## OctoAcme Project Management Overview

OctoAcme follows a structured project management lifecycle designed to ensure consistent, repeatable project execution across all teams. Here's how we run projects:

### **Initiation**
Every project starts with clear definition of purpose, desired outcomes, stakeholders, and success criteria. We capture initial scope and establish the approvals required before moving to planning. This phase ensures alignment and sets realistic expectations.

### **Planning**
During planning, we translate project outcomes into actionable plans covering scope, schedule, milestones, resource allocation, risk identification, and communication strategies. We use lightweight, reusable templates stored in the `docs/` folder to maintain consistency and accelerate planning cycles.

### **Execution & Tracking**
We track all work against established milestones, maintain a single source of truth for tasks and status, and run regular check-ins to keep the team synchronized. Progress is communicated transparently through defined channels.

### **Risk & Communication**
Risks are identified, logged, and actively managed using escalation paths defined in our process documents. Stakeholder communications are concise, timely, and keep decision-makers informed of status, issues, and next steps.

### **Release & Deployment**
Releases follow standardized checklists and sign-off procedures. We coordinate release windows, manage rollback plans, and ensure smooth deployments through disciplined change management.

### **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement**
After significant milestones or releases, we conduct retrospectives to reflect on what went well, what could be improved, and what we learned. Validated improvements are fed back into this documentation repository, ensuring our processes evolve with our experience.
